## What to Look For

When choosing your first VPS provider, consider these factors:

1. **Price** - How much per month?
2. **Reliability** - Do they have good uptime?
3. **Support** - Can you get help when needed?
4. **Locations** - Where are their data centers?
5. **Easy to use** - Is their control panel beginner-friendly?
